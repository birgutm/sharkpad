Phytochemical and Ethnobotanical Database

It has been proved throughout human history, that plants have been major source for medicine. Huge amount of human intelligence and times have been consumed just to collect those valuable information especially in one of the oldest megadiversity country such as Malaysia. Biodiversity Informatics field offers more systematic management upon utilization of ethobotanical data to keep up with rapid development of information technologies and multidisciplinary approach of data archival.
